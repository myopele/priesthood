How to Use This Course

Each module includes principles, practices, and a completion checklist. The goal is not information alone. The goal is alignment, conduct, spiritual discipline, divination readiness, and the living expression of SGI principles.

Three SGI Paths

  • A — Ilekes Path: identity, devotion, authorization, spiritual orientation, and alignment.
  • B — Warriors Path: discipline, ethical speech, purification, courage, and spiritual defense.
  • C — Hand of Ifá: wisdom, destiny alignment, ancestral intelligence, divination readiness, and sacred responsibility.

The Course Modules

Open each module, complete the checklist, and your progress updates automatically on this device.

Completion + Integrity

Completion means you can explain how the Tree changes your conduct, speech, divination practice, and daily choices. In SGI terms, knowledge must become character.

Completion Commitments

  • Radical Devotion: I commit to devotion that becomes visible through action.
  • Words of Power: I commit to speech that heals, clarifies, protects, and aligns.
  • Infinite Wisdom: I commit to wisdom that is lived, practiced, and verified.
  • Royal Legacy: I commit to extending the dignity, beauty, and responsibility of ancestral inheritance.
  • Enlightened Ancestors: I commit to standing on the shoulders of those who carried light before me.
